THIS IS QUICKMOBILE

More than a mobile event app, we make experiences meaningful.

In the beginning.

QuickMobile was started in a basement by two friends who shared a vision of sending messages using their mobile devices. With the dawn of the iPhone, the duo’s vision grew into the world’s very first mobile event app.

Today we're different, but still the same.

Since then, we’ve grown into the trusted industry leader, delivering a mobile event platform that is used around the world to create meaningful meetings and events. Our offices may be bigger but we’ve always held onto the spirit with which we began: be genuine, listen to customers, and keep it simple.

We’re redefining events.

QuickMobile is much more than an event app. Designed for today’s mobile-savvy attendee, our mobile event app platform creates meaningful and lasting experiences. We help our customers deliver personalized, productive and fully-integrated events and meetings—across the enterprise and beyond—that captivate and engage attendees. With unparalleled expertise and a commitment to best practices, QuickMobile makes it happen.

You’ve got our undivided attention.

Simply put, you are our first and only priority. Following in the footsteps of the Ritz-Carlton’s Gold Standard of customer service, we promise to listen and engage with you every step of the way—from our very first conversation to your 1,000th event with us. Our mission is to help you, by every means possible, to create a meaningful, immersive experience for your attendees.

We care about giving back.

We prefer trees to paper and relish intelligence at our fingertips. At QuickMobile, we are committed to advancing change and challenging the status quo by providing unprecedented approaches to sustainability and community in everything we do. We donate to WeForest.org, an international non-profit association with the following mission: to create and promote a pioneer movement in large-scale sustainable reforestation.
