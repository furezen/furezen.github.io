Security

Serving global enterprises with the most secure event app platform available.

REQUEST DEMO

It’s no wonder over 50 of the Fortune 100 trust QuickMobile.

We deliver the only enterprise-grade secured event app platform that scales across your global infrastructure, providing the highest degree of data integrity and availability with the confidence you need to launch your next big event.

Create flexible and configurable passwords

Choose your level of password security and define rules for password reset for any type of meeting or event. You can also secure the entire app or specific app features to ensure you safeguard confidential information.

Enforce company policies

Make sure your attendees accept corporate disclaimers, privacy policies, copyright notices or legal notifications prior to accessing the app. Plus you can launch your own opt-in policies and capture the results for compliance purposes.

Keep event content confidential

Take complete control of your event app content: publish sessions for select groups of attendees, configure rules for attendee login, control attendee profile sharing and secure the distribution of documents from the app.

Distribute apps and store data privately

Choose either a private or public distribution of your app and store your data in a regional data center that complies with corporate privacy and security requirements.
