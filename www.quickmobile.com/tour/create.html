You’re in the driver’s seat.

Since our first event app (THE first, actually) was built in 2008, we've learned a lot about how to make it easy to get content in so you can get the best results out—ensuring you’re in full control of the experience.

Configure

We designed our platform to fit the way you work, with roles and permissions that let you assign responsibility across your team and event with flexible role- and permission-based access.

Act

From schedules and room locations to speakers and countless other details, things can change on a dime in the world of events. You have plenty of tools at your disposal to instantly moderate discussions, make instant updates, remove comments, push messages or take a poll.

Analyze

Every tap of the app creates a data point that can tell you how successful your event is. We give you the insight you need, when you need it.
