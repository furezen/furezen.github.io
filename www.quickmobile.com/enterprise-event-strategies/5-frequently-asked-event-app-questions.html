5 Frequently Asked Event App Questions

by QuickMobile
December 11, 2013

The event app industry is growing fast and furiously, but there is still a lot of mystery surrounding this technology. We were recently at the Financial and Insurance Conference Planners annual meeting, where we gave a workshop on mobile event apps.

We were asked a lot of great questions at the conference, but these weren’t new questions unique to the finance and insurance space. We’ve heard many of these queries from event planners in nearly every sector, so we thought it would be helpful to share the answers to the five questions we get asked the most:

1. Isn’t an event app just a digital agenda?

In short, no. Unlike a printed agenda, an event app is a dynamic tool that creates new opportunities for personalization, connection and engagement, while also giving event planners the flexibility to make changes on-the-fly, open new revenue streams and gather real-time audience and participation data.

Also, with an event app, you get a 365-day communication channel that’s in every purse and pocket, providing both event organizers and attendees with new ways to build relationships year-round.

But that’s just the tip of the iceberg so give these other resources a read for more information or feel free to reach out to us directly with any questions.

2. How do I create an app that will deliver value not just to attendees, but my organization too?

Creating a successful event app that excites attendees and provides valuable returns to your organization starts with a well thought out event app strategy.

This involves giving careful thought to who your audience is and what motivates them to attend your event. You also need to consider what your business and event goals are – Is it to increase brand awareness? Generate new revenue? Amplify your social media reach?

Once you know these things, you can begin to build your strategy. To help make this a less daunting task, we recommend using our P.O.S.T. Mobile Event Strategy worksheet. It makes the process of thinking about what you want to accomplish much simpler because it breaks your strategy down into just four areas: People, Objectives, Strategy and Technology.

3. Can the app be used for smaller events?

Yes, event apps can be used for events and meetings of all sizes! Whether you use a single-event app or a multi-event app, your app vendor should be able to customize the features to suit your event’s unique needs and requirements.

4. Can an event app be used to track continuing education units (CEUs)?

Yes, you can track CEUs using either a Check-In or Lead Retrieval feature. With Check-In, the attendee simply checks into the session using the app to register their attendance. Lead Retrieval requires a room monitor or staff member to scan each attendee’s badge, which should have a bar code or QR code on it, as they come into a session. Both methods register that the attendee was at the session so that the right credits will be applied towards their CEUs.

5. How do I make sure my attendees will use the app?

Simply having an event app doesn’t guarantee that your attendees will download it or even know that it exists. Proper promotion of your app is key to getting high download and usage rates. In our Best Practices for Event App Promotion webinar, we discuss tactics you can use before, during, and after your event to keep attendees interested in using your event app.

You may also find this Event App Promotion cheat sheet handy. Use it to quickly determine when’s the best time to perform your different promotion activities.
