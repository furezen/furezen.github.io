Must Haves for Enterprise Event Apps

by QuickMobile
October 19, 2016

Event organizers everywhere are turning to enterprise event apps for a number of reasons, including having full control over branding and functionality, scalability and more. But one of the key draws of using an event app is the rich data that can be tracked and measured to gain important insights into an event’s performance.

Contrary to past years when event organizers relied on elementary performance metrics such as number of attendees, email database opt-ins, and so on, event apps have the power to track and measure so much more. For instance, some of the actionable insights event app analytics provide include how people engage with your app and its content – giving you information on what attracts and engages attendees most. It also offers individual attendee activity so you know who’s influencing, who’s following, and who’s sitting on the sidelines – giving you to the opportunity to improve engagement. You can even see who attends your event, be it a keynote speaker session or booth at your tradeshow, along with individual names and contact information. You can also send event attendees personalized emails or even group in-app notifications to alert them about an upcoming event.

The opportunities to attract, engage with, and delight attendees at events are endless. Enterprise event apps can help you make informed decisions based on big data to ensure attendee satisfaction and overall event success.

3-Step Strategy for Event Success

Here are a few easy steps to ensure you host a successful event:

Step 1: Define your goals

Think about what you and your executives would define as a successful event. What are the goals and why are they important? It’s paramount that you outline S.M.A.R.T. (specific, measurable, agreed upon, realistic, and time-bound) goals at the outset of your event planning process to safeguard your event’s success.
