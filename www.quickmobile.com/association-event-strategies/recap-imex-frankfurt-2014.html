Recap: IMEX Frankfurt 2014

by QuickMobile
May 23, 2014

For a number of years the QuickMobile team has supported IMEX in delivering an excellent mobile event app experience to its participants. This year IMEX Frankfurt really took it to a new level of engagement and, dare we say it, captivation!

Not only could exhibitors see their diaries, but they could also manage them, move appointments around and send messages easily to the buyers. The busy hosted buyers could see their schedules, find their next location on the map, take notes and share the stories of their experience on social media as they moved through an intensive learning immersion.  

In our exhibition booth, we built on the #IMEXselfie experience with a free-hanging “be in your app” experience, and we had a ton of fun with dozens of photos being shared online and with the guests who stopped by. And as guests arrived at the gala, we amped this experience up. Guests could have their own celebrity step-and-repeat photo taken (see the gala album here) as they arrived on the red carpet, adding to the euphoria surrounding this always special event. Together with the Meeting Design Institute, we were able to be part of the ongoing commentary of the meeting community as we participated in capturing interviews that took advantage of all the thought leaders who attended.

We also released the findings from the 1st annual Global Event Planning and Mobile Event Technology at the show. The report was produced by QuickMobile and the IMEX Group, and the results were derived from a survey of international meeting planners conducted by Insights West on our behalf earlier this year.

Like all of you, we go to IMEX for its connections and unparalleled opportunity to spend face-to-face time. While we build mobile apps and understand innately the power of everyone being connected through the devices ubiquitously with them, we believe wholly in the power of taking these virtual connections offline and deepening them when you are physically in the same space.  

Thank you to all of you who connected with us in Frankfurt! We appreciate being selected for appointments, and enjoy hearing how we can help enhance meeting experiences – which we can do in 15 languages, wherever they may be happening around the world!
