From the Planner’s Desk: PCMA EduCon 2015 Recap

by QuickMobile
July 3, 2015

Another leader for professional development of the people in our industry is certainly PCMA, and we were pleased to once again be supporting them at EduCon, their mid-year offering which brings together hundreds of professionals for learning and sharing, in ways both expected and those unexpected.

The mobile app we created allowed for easy polling and capturing in real time what was happening in the audience. This worked exceptionally well in meeting the learning objectives and being able to adjust as needed. 

In a low-tech crowd-sourcing session on the final morning, the giant post-its were out as the crowd offered topics they would lead sessions on, and what they wanted to learn more about. These post-its went up on the wall and the decisions were made before lunch, the session spaces were allocated and the details of where you could find your learning were all in the app before the sessions started so it was easy to choose what you wanted to participate in.

It truly is these smart, thoughtful integrations with mobile that are not the future of meetings, but are enhancing meetings and events now, and the reality of what we need to understand to best accomplish our objectives being set for our meetings. Mobile fits into meetings of all sizes from just a few to a few hundred to several thousand, and we’re always happy to talk to you about how easy it is to incorporate mobile into your meeting design.
