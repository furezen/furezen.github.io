Convening Leaders Mobile Highlights

by Trevor Roald
January 9, 2014  | 

Convening Leaders 2014 is poised to be a hot-bed for innovation this year! PCMA has really embraced the ‘[R]evolution’ theme and is pushing boundaries with numerous highlights and experiments that all event professionals (and nerds) can geek-out on. It’s nice to see an industry association being bold and taking risks for the betterment of their members and the meetings industry.

From a mobile event app and technology perspective, there are a number of cool things happening at Convening Leaders 2014. As you’re finalizing your schedule and packing your bags for Boston, be sure to add the following mobile highlights to your calendar:

Download the convening leaders app

PCMA has stepped up their mobile event app game for 2014 by partnering with QuickMobile. Their custom event app is jam packed with useful features that will help attendees maximize their experience at Convening Leaders. Be sure to download the app to get full access to all the PCMA action.

Visit the PCMA solutions kiosks

Centrally located near event registration are the PCMA Solutions Kiosks. These are companies that PCMA has hand selected to showcase their best-in-class event solutions. QuickMobile will have a prominent presence here and will be demonstrating their cutting edge mobile event apps. Be sure to come by to speak directly with the global leaders and see the latest and greatest mobile event solutions!

Attended techcentral mobile session

George Hines, CIO of Viad Corp (GES’s parent company and Sponsor of the PCMA Mobile App), will be sharing his wealth of technical and business acumen in his TechCentral session: Improving the Meeting Experience with Mobile and Wireless Technology. In his bio, George says he’s inspired by the John Cage quote: “Nothing is a mistake. There’s no win and no fail. There’s only make,” so expect a no-nonsense, outcome oriented session that will leave you with a better understanding of the current event technology landscape and the role technology will play in the future attendee experience.

Learn about multi-touchpoint event marketing

Integrated marketing is key for any successful event program. Suzanne Carawan, CMO of, the ASAE endorsed marketing agency, HighRoad Solution is a true expert on combining traditional, social and mobile media to produce superior marketing results. Her session: Multi-Touchpoint Event Marketing: Blending Digital & Live for Maximum Impact is a do-not-miss session for anyone interested in learning more about both strategic and tactical applications of mobile technology for marketing purposes.

Witness hackers in action

Hackathon style events are emerging as a popular meeting format and PCMA has embraced the trend by experimenting with it at Convening Leaders. In the experiment, PCMA will be hosting the first ever PCMA Hackathon in the Learning Lounge 2.0. The Hackathon, sponsored by QuickMobile, PCMA and Freeman XP, will pit Boston area students and software developers against each other as they attempt to produce the best mobile app solution to address the challenge to [R]evolutionize Connectivity at event. There is $5,000 in prize money available so the hacking awesomeness is bound to be great.

Convening Leaders attendees will be able to visit the InnovationZone and Hacker Workroom in the Learning Lounge 2.0 and pullback the curtain to see first-hand what goes into developing a hackathon and how the model can be applied to their own events.

PCMA achievement awards

Each year PCMA honors outstanding members for their contributions to the meetings industry by awarding Achievement Awards from the Convening Leaders main stage. We are truly honored to be the recipient of this year’s Innovation Award. The award recognizes strategic, results-focused innovative ideas by an individual or organization in the meetings and hospitality industry.

Cookie Walner, CMP, CMM, CAE, CEM, PCMA’s Achievement Awards Committee Chair, commented, “It is exciting to see the innovation and fun that QuickMobile’s apps can bring to a conference. The financial savings are significant, eliminating the need for printing and the reduction in paper and distribution costs. And this innovation collects significant data and measurable insights for an organization. Congratulations, QuickMobile.”

Patrick Payne, CEO, QuickMobile, will be receiving the Innovation Award at the Closing Luncheon at 12:00 PM, Wednesday January 15th – be sure to be there to share in the excitement and celebrate mobile as a growing and integral part of the meetings industry.
