From the Planner’s Desk: The FRESH Conference 2015

by QuickMobile
July 8, 2015

I recently had the opportunity (at last!) to participate in the Fresh Conference. This innovative event began with the Meeting Design Institute having the idea that meeting design is its own discipline and requires fresh thinking from forward-looking individuals, and bringing together thought leaders from around the world to share these is the best way to achieve this.

While I have many takeaways from this event, the key for mobile is simple – integration is key. For this event there was a great website, a mobile app, a scheduling tool, voting tool in the app plus separate voting for the awards and some sessions, and a networking tool which comes within the website – all separated. Some of this was done for showcasing reasons, but for participant ease it was not ideal and disrupted the usefulness. It makes me think there are many conferences that do not have the opportunity to fully integrate a digital and mobile strategy, and this is a challenge, which QuickMobile is working to help meeting professionals overcome.

It starts with identifying the technology you are using, often at minimum a website, registration system, membership and scheduling, and a learning management or marketing tool. For various reasons including populating the app and obtaining manageable and relevant analytics, such as who attended what sessions or who “checked in” with various sponsors or exhibitors, you will need to truly consider what you have, what you need to accomplish and what your mobile app provider can support you with.

The less taps a participant has to make, the less windows they have to open to participate in what you have identified as “positive meeting behaviours,” which means you’ll have better results to enjoy. Talk to us – this is what we understand and are here to guide you on!
