How to Encourage Engagement During Your Event

Engagement at Every Stage - Part 2 of 3

by Grace Cheung
November 2, 2016

During an event, mobile event apps provide ways for attendees to engage—with one another and with presenters—in ways that are difficult to replicate with anything else.

Activities that promote audience engagement while the event is ongoing, like gamification, live-polling and Q&A, open the door to two-way communication that encourages attendees to participate and add their voices to the conversation. Your events become more meaningful and memorable to individual attendees when those attendees are able to find personalized experiences.

Last week, we unveiled the secret to motivating attendees before the event has even begun with mobile event apps. In this second part of our blog series, “Engagement at Every Stage,” we’ll show you how to take advantage of event apps to encourage attendees to engage throughout the event.

Keeping Attendees In-the-Moment

A key part of keeping attendees engaged is keeping them in-the-moment: making sure they are interested and engrossed in the proceedings, and motivating them to participate.

Helping attendees stay abreast of what is happening at the event is a big part of that. This can be as simple as sharing updates of schedule changes as they occur, reminding attendees that they have access to a global shared schedule, or sending push notifications when an important activity is coming up.

Without these tactics, it can be easy to get lost in the busy pace of enterprise events. Directing attendees’ focus to significant moments and activities as they happen will encourage attendees to participate by reminding them of the upcoming opportunities that they might otherwise miss out on.

For example, announcing the leaders of the in-app game at crucial points during the session will encourage attendees to join in and compete before the game ends. This is a great resource to help you learn more about using gamification to drive engagement at conferences and events.

Connecting Attendees with Presenters and Each Other

According to Inc., face-to-face events are considered one of the best places to build business relationships, and mobile event apps facilitate attendees’ ability to connect with each other as well as with presenters.

In-app messaging expedites networking and communication between attendees, and can make inter- or intra- enterprise connections all the more far-reaching. Taking advantage of in-app social media not only improves communication but also invites attendees to become co-creators of the event experience. This can be as simple as creating and promoting official hashtags encouraging attendees to utilize them on internal and external social media. Helping attendees build community with other like-minded attendees makes it easier for attendees to make stronger, more memorable connections.

Methods of live engagement—such as quizzes, audience surveys, or Q&A—allow presenters to seamlessly ask for audience input, making it easy to continually adapt to the attendees’ needs. Interaction with the presenter can help attendees make the presentation their own, and encourages them to stay interested and motivated to participate.

With a mobile event app, there are countless opportunities for attendees to take part in the conversation, turning presentations into memorable learning experiences and allowing audiences to co-create their event experience.
