Why QuickMobile?

At QuickMobile, we are turning mobile event technology on its head. We’re blazing a new trail, redefining mobile event apps to set a new standard for meaningful attendee engagement. And we want you to come help us make it happen.

We are so much more than an event app company. Our mission is to help our customers ensure every attendee has the most meaningful experience possible, whether they’re at the Monday morning review meeting or a week-long trade show extravaganza. QuickMobile delivers a personalized, collaborative event experience unlike anything they’ve experienced before.

Want to be part of the revolution? We’re committed to transforming meetings and events across the enterprise and beyond—and we’d love you to join us!

Culture

A healthy work-life balance is what all companies promise. But at QuickMobile, we live it. We work hard but we make having fun a priority. And we make sure you have the time and energy to have a life outside of the office. Come see why QuickMobile employees are smiling.

Perks & Benefits

We enjoy perks like a social lounge with a big screen TV, ping pong table, and couches for chillin'—or power naps. And you’ll love our regular happy hours and social events, including the occasional bowlerama! We offer referral bonuses and ample opportunities to advance your career through education and training.

Health & Wellness

We take wellness very seriously. In addition to our comprehensive health benefits package, you can take advantage of our in-house weekly massages and standing desks. Keep fit by running, biking, or walking the nearby seawall or enjoy discounts at a nearby fitness facility.

Learning & Development

We offer all kinds of in-house training to develop new skills. We also provide online tutorials from Lynda.com where you can learn technology, creative, and business skills to help advance your career. And with our clients spread across the globe, you have opportunities to travel to conferences and events in new and exciting locales.

Amazing Office

Our beautiful head office is in the heart of downtown Vancouver, where suit-and-ties are optional (okay, totally unnecessary) and the ocean and city views are inspirational. We have quick access to the Stanley Park sea wall for lunchtime runs and a secure underground bike cage—plus some of the best restaurants Vancouver has to offer, just steps away.
